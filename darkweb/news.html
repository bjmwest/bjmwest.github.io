Breaking: West responds to East declaration of war

12 AUG 2020

On August 11 at 8:15pm, the natives of the fanghorn and forbidden forests declared their intentions for independence from us. We disagreed with their beliefs but respected them all the same. A day passed of peaceful relations and coexistence. Unfortunately, peace is not what they desired. Around 10:40am, we were suddenly and deliberately attacked by the militants of the forests, which resulted in several Western casualties. We had no choice but to consider them hostile and defend the life and liberty of the Citizens of West.
