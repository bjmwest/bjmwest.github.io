Ultra One

What is Ultra One?

The Ultra One is a revolutionary new cycle training device, using ultrasonic technology it gives precise, real time feedback of the distance to the rider in front, enabling you to fine tune your drafting skills or practice riding consistently at a safe distance. This concept is patent pending in the UK (GB 1522031.2).

So how does it work?

The Ultra One is a compact device that attaches to the handlebars using a standard Cateye mount. Just set a target distance in cm (bear in mind the device measures to the saddle of the rider in front, not wheel to wheel) and start riding. The clear, bright LED display tells you your exact distance, whilst the two coloured LEDs tell you at a glance if you’re on target or not. You can also set a margin of error for your target, or you can just hold the Ultra One at the desired distance and let it set the target/margin of error for you. The Ultra One has a simple, intuitive three button interface that allows for easy, on-the-fly adjustment. Once your ride is over you can download your performance data onto a computer for analysis; the Ultra One can store up to 17 hours worth of data, logged every second.
